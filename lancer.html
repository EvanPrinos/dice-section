Lancer

Originating with Dungeons and Dragons, but quickly growing to fit a variety of loved genres, tabletop role-playing games, or TTRPGs are well known for their associations with dice, but many of the systems I've played still work to approach or encourage a wide variety of different gameplay patterns.

Through this site we'll be inspecting some of the decisions that divide these systems, specifically prioritizing the ones I've played and feel I understand. I hope that in 10 years, looking back on this site, I'll have an entire list of new systems I'm not as familiar with.
